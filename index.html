Stop Cyber Bullying

Cyberbullying is when people use the internet to be mean or hurtful.

Remember to use technology kindly and never cyberbully!

Examples of Cyber Bullying

CyberStalking

Cyberstalking is when a cyberbully watches someone online very closely.

Outing

When a cyberbully shares a person's secret without permission to hurt or embarrass them.

Doxxing

When a cyberbully shares private info, like someone's address or school online to harm or intimidate.

Flaming

When a cyberbully sends insults and profanity to a victim to provoke an online fight.

Trickery

A cyberbully can befriend a victim, gaining their trust and tricking them into sharing secrets.

Understanding Bullying

Cyberbullying is bullying that takes place over digital devices like cell phones, computers, and tablets.

Effects of Cyberbullying

Cyberbullying can affect everyone—those who are bullied, those who bully, and those who witness bullying.
